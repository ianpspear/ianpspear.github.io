Finding the Vertex of a Parabola

To find the vertex of a parabola, you can use the following steps:

  1. Find the x-coordinate of the vertex using the formula: x = -b/2a.
  2. Substitute the x-coordinate of the vertex back into the original equation to find the y-coordinate of the vertex.
  3. The vertex of the parabola is the point (x, y).

The vertex is the point where the parabola changes direction. The x-coordinate of the vertex is also called the axis of symmetry.
